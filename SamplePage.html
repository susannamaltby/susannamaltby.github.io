Is 'Chia Seed Water' Good For You?

Social mediaproponents claim it has digestive and weight loss benefits. Heres what experts think.

When asked if any studies had examined whether chia seeds could reduce constipation, the experts said they werent aware of any. But theres good research in general showing that dietary fiber an essential nutrient abundant in chia seeds can help.

A two-tablespoon serving of dry chia seeds contains nearly 10 grams of fiber. Thats more than twice the amount in a medium Red Delicious apple, and a good portion of the recommended 21 to 38 grams most people should eat each day.
